Administrative Details

Our client is The Sign, the school paper fort the Institute of Design (NTNU).

Purpose and goals

The purpose of the website is to show the public who the editorial staff of TheSign is, what they do, and their schedule. This means presenting the editorial staff, showing off the different issues of the school paper and the process of making these, and lastly, informing them of the release date for the upcoming issues. Additionally, the public will have an easy way to get in contact with the editorial staff. The goal is to reach out to a larger public, and in that way increase the number of readers of TheSign.

Audience

The intended audience of the website will consist mainly of students and teachers at NTNU, especially those from the Institute of Design. The newspaper TheSign contains information that is largely directed at design students. However, the information may still be attractive to students from other affiliations, and are therefore included in the intended audience. Many students might not have been able to attend the official release of an issue, and might therefore be interested in checking it out online, and maybe downloading it from this website. Some users might even be interested in viewing some of the older editions.

The intended audience will also consist of potentially new members of the editorial staff. Once a year the editorial staff of TheSign looks for new members. The website will be a good way of promoting the group and their work, and in this way maybe convince several students to apply for the available positions.

The last part of the intended audience consists of potentially new sponsors.The magazine is constantly in need of new sponsors. With a website they may easily show potential sponsors what TheSign is and what they might agree to sponsor.

The content of the site and how it is organized

About

This section will present information about TheSign, and a brief introduction of the editorial staff, providing the user with photos of the current members.

Contact us

This page is intended for students, teachers or sponsors who wish to get in touch with the staff. The page will include a form that users can fill in. Students will, for example, be able to write an application for available positions in this form.

Landing page

The different issues will be presented on the landing page, with a picture of the front cover and a small description. Each issue can separately be clicked on for more information.The landing page might be restricted to only show the four to five latest issues, and instead produce a button at the bottom of the page which gives the user the opportunity to view older issues.

Issue page

Each issue will have its own page where the user will be able to access a preview. The page will include a short summary of the content of the currently selected issue and some of the illustrations and photos that can be found inside. Users can download the PDF version by pressing the “download” button at the bottom of the page.

Specific requirements

  • The system should be designed in a way so that the required information is easily accessible at all times.
  • The system must be capable of being updated with every new release.
  • The system must be easy to navigate.
  • The website must be in norwegian.
  • The information written in the form on the contact page must be sent to the admin, when the user clicks on the “send” button.
  • The pictures must have a size limit, so that the pictures appear quickly when the website is visited.
  • The background and fonts must provide a clean and professional look to the website.
